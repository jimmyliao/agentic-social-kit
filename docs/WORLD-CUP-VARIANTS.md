> capability** — same engine, zero new core. **Generic only** (use "Red/Blue team",

## Why a topical (World Cup) skin is strategically good — not just a gimmick
1. **Timeliness** — World Cup is live → blog rides the trend → much higher GDE shareability.
2. **★ Makes the safety-gate story vivid** — social between *rival fans*: the gate
   **downgrades a "wants to trash-talk (high intensity)" into friendly banter**, and
   requires consent before connecting → "even cross-rivalry can socialize low-pressure,
   safely" = the most cinematic framing of the governance guardrail.
3. **Adds a "shared-event stream" dimension** the generic Mixer lacks — a **goal →
   triggers a social burst** (everyone reacts) → perfect to **demo the SDK's `triggers`**
   (event-driven; we haven't shown this yet) → one more technical highlight for the blog.
4. **Very cheap** — pure re-skin + add a "match event" trigger; engine fully reused,
   zero confidential content, public-safe.

## Scope discipline (don't over-build)
- This is a **thematic variant / demo flourish of The Mixer**, NOT a real World Cup app.
- **Core deliverable stays the generic version** (clean, teachable Sprint sample);
  the topical skin is a **bonus** that makes the blog/demo catchier.
- Keep it fully generic (Red/Blue team, no real-brand IP).

  "safe in high-privacy corporate environments" — office World-Cup pool, avatars
  socialize but the gate locks everything to workplace-appropriate = most on-theme,
  most persuasive.
- **For a moving GIF / emergent story → 💛 Cross-rivalry friendship arc** (the memory
  ladder crossing a rivalry; great short-clip material).

## The Mixer's own narrative (for reference, the generic base)
One line: *a few strangers' AI avatars in a lounge autonomously decide whether/how to
socialize — but every step passes a **declarative safety gate**, so no one gets
bothered or crossed.* Arc: 5 strangers w/ personalities → system finds who clicks →
LLM persona proposes a social action (share a drink / gentle intro / leave a note) →
**gate governs each step** (outgoing Jordan's "clink glasses (5)" with shy Theo →
over cap → **downgraded to "leave a friendly note"**; consent-needed → ASK_USER) →
relationships form (stranger→acquaintance→friend→close). **Moral**: "your digital twin
can make friends for you, but never oversteps" — autonomous social = low-pressure,
consent-first, you're in control. **The safety gate is the protagonist.**
